## What our audio runtime must support

- Separate buses for ambient, reel-loop, topper/feature FX, overlay stings, and counting ticks.
- Per-reel stop cues keyed off explicit reel-stop events, not a single generic settle callback.
- One-shot + loop layering for boost states: ignition, sustained burn/electricity, and hit accents.
- Randomized variation pools for lightning, bonus coin entry/fly/hit, and line-win cues.
- Modal open and close routing independent from gameplay reel audio.

## Unknowns

- Exact donor mix levels, ducking rules, and sidechain behavior are `UNOBSERVED`.
- Exact mapping from every cue to every visible event is not fully isolated in the public evidence, so filename-based mappings remain `INFERENCE` unless supported by a reconciled clip or explicit trigger package.
