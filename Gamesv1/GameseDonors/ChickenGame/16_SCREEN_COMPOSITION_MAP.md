## Composition takeaways for Engineering

- The donor composition is not a flat background plus reels. It is a stacked stage: background -> cabinet -> topper/jackpots -> HUD/control rails -> modal/win overlays.
- The topper and jackpot plaques are structurally part of the cabinet composition, not independent decorative side widgets.
- The buy-bonus modal is the clearest example of donor depth: dimmed gameplay below, tier cards centered above, and footer bet controls still readable.
