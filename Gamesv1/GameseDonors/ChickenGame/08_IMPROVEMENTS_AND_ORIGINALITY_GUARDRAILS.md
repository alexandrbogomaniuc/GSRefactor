## Originality guardrails (non-negotiable)
- Do not copy donor character art, symbol art, color palette, typography, lighting style, UI geometry, or animation curves.
- Mechanics can inspire design, but final audiovisual direction must be original and brand-distinct.
- Use unique world/theme language (new setting, iconography, coin motif, and narrative wrapper).
- Build all promo and in-game assets from original source files; donor captures are reference-only evidence.

## Recommended unique direction (for our product)
- Theme direction: original high-energy “electro foundry” or “mythic forge” economy loop, not farm/chicken thematic reuse.
- Art style: bolder geometric iconography and cleaner modern motion language; avoid donor silhouette language.
- UX direction: stronger tutorial readability (section tabs for Collect/Boost/Bonus), better control labels, and clearer accessibility hierarchy.
