# Symbol Selection Rationale (Phase 1)

This document identifies the 6 representative symbols chosen for the Phase 1 conceptual contact sheet based *strictly* on the actual `atlas_symbols.json` contract for the OpenAI provider in Game 7000.

## Contract Anchors

The 6 chosen symbols, ordered by priority of importance for game readability:

### 1. Wild Symbol
- **Contract Key**: `symbol-8-bolt`
- **Actual Role**: Wild (Substitutes for pays)
- **Selection Rationale**: Wilds are highly visible and often animated. We are interpreting "bolt" in the rustic context as a shiny **Brass Weather Vane Lightning Bolt/Rooster**.
- **Risks**: Must be distinct enough from the top premium rooster so they don't visually merge on the reels.
- **Material Notes**: Highly polished brass, catching warm sunlight.

### 2. Scatter / Collector Symbol
- **Contract Key**: `collector-symbol`
- **Actual Role**: Feature Trigger / Collector
- **Selection Rationale**: The most critical feature trigger. We interpret "collector" as a **Golden Barn Door / Coop Entrance**. 
- **Risks**: Must have a strong, commanding silhouette.
- **Material Notes**: Rich mahogany wood with heavy golden/brass hinges and a glowing interior.

### 3. Bonus / Feature Symbol
- **Contract Key**: `symbol-7-coin`
- **Actual Role**: Bonus / Hold & Win Coin
- **Selection Rationale**: The core symbol of the Hold & Win feature. We interpret the "coin" directly as a **Shiny Engraved Brass Coin or Golden Egg Coin**.
- **Risks**: Cannot look flat; must feel thick, valuable, and tactile.
- **Material Notes**: Gleaming gold/brass, embossed with a rooster or wheat motif.

### 4. Highest-Value Premium Symbol
- **Contract Key**: `symbol-9-rooster`
- **Actual Role**: Top Payout Premium 1
- **Selection Rationale**: The title character.
- **Risks**: Anatomical weirdness in AI generation. Must look slightly "crazy" (per theme) but not terrifying or mutated.
- **Material Notes**: Vibrant organic feathers (reds, oranges, golds) to contrast heavily against the wooden and brass UI.

### 5. Strongest Representative Mid-Value Symbol
- **Contract Key**: `symbol-5-bar`
- **Actual Role**: Mid Value 3
- **Selection Rationale**: The traditional "Bar" symbol translates perfectly to rustic materials without losing casino legibility. We interpret this as a **Stack of Polished Wood and Brass Blocks**.
- **Risks**: Can look like literal firewood if not polished enough. Must look like a casino asset.
- **Material Notes**: Lacquered wood, brass edges, etched lettering.

### 6. Strongest Representative Low-Value Symbol
- **Contract Key**: `symbol-0-egg`
- **Actual Role**: Low Value 1
- **Selection Rationale**: It is the foundational low-paying symbol and directly ties into the farm/rooster theme. We use a **Clean Painted Farm Egg**.
- **Risks**: Can appear too boring or blend into the reel background. Needs strong shading.
- **Material Notes**: Matte cream or pale blue shell, subtle organic speckling, strong studio lighting.

---
**Why these six?**
They cover the entire spectrum of materials (organic, wood, metal, painted) and values (wild, feature, premium, mid, low) dictated by the contract, giving the clearest possible preview of the final game's readability.
