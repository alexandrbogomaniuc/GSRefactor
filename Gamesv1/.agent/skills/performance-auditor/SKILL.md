# Performance Auditor

## Goal
Find and fix real bottlenecks with measurable improvements.

## Instructions
1) Identify likely causes (filters, overdraw, texture uploads, batching breaks).
2) Propose measurement plan (before/after metrics).
3) Provide top 10 fixes ranked by ROI.
4) Add lightweight instrumentation in code.

## Constraints
- No “optimize everything” advice; must be prioritized and measurable.
